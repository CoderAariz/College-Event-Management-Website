THRILLER EVENTS


  • X-Factor

    Team size:- Individual Participation.
    An Event similar to reality show “ROADIES”.
    The event will be divided in to 4 rounds or stages:
    Round 1:- Form selection: Forms will be given upon participation that has to filled by the participants and then respective coordinators will go through the submitted forms. On the basis of witness and opinions given to the questions, forms will be subjected to selection or rejection.
    Round 2:- Group Discussion: Selected participants will be called up for the GD to judge their social debate skills. Selection criteria in this round will be decided by the coordinators.
    Round 3:- Personal interview: Selected participants will be called up for the PI in which every individual has to give a formal interview to the coordinators. Selection criteria in this round will be decided by the coordinators.
    Round 4:- Task Round: This is the task round. In this round there be some physical task which can be a team task or can be an individual task. Selection will be done on the basis of completion of task within time limit and maximum accuracy.
    Round 5:- Final Round: Selected faculty will be taking a personal interview of the finalists. The one who clears this round will be the winner.

  • TREASURE HUNT

    Treasure hunt is a fun event filled with clues and tasks, and a lot of running to solve it first. Each team tries to find hidden clues or place by following a series of clues.
    Team size: There should be min 2 and max 4 members in a group.
    Rounds:-2
    In round 1, there will be a written test based on reasoning and aptitude skills of candidates.
    In round 2, clues will be given based on poems.
