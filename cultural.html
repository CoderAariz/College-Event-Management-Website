  • NATRAJ(Dance)

    Entries:- Solo,Duet,Group(5-6 members)
    Rounds: Audition and face off
    Auditions: Participants are required to perform on any song of their personal choice. The event heads & judges will select the performers.
    Face Off:The selected individual or groups are required to perform to challenge their opponent. And the better one will compete with the next group.

  • FACE PAINTING

    Team size:Maximum 2 members.
    Rounds:1
    Time Limit:1 hour
    In this event participant have to do painting on partners face according to the theme.

  • T-SHIRT PAINTING

    Team size:Maximum 2 members.
    Rounds:1
    Time Limit: 1 hour
    In this the participants have to bring a plain t- shirt and the corresponding material used for painting. They have to paint the t-shirt according to the theme provided to them within the given span of time.

  • Rangoli

    Team size:Maximum 5 members.
    Rounds: 1
    Time Limit:45 minutes
    Participants will be provided with some social issues from which they have to choose one and make a simple or 3-D Rangoli. After completing Rangoli, judgement will be given and questions will be asked from each team by the respective judges.

  • Mehandi

    Team size: Maximum 2 members.
    Rounds: 1
    Time Limit: 1 hour
    In this event participants have to design mehndi according to the theme.
